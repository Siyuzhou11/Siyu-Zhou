Project 1: Distributed Control of Microgrids for Reliable, Efficient and Resilient Operation (Funded by Office of Naval Research)

  • Developed a power buffer system for microgrids using Matlab/Simulink to compensate for transients during disturbances.
  • Designed a distributed, event-triggered resilient control method for DC-DC boost and buck converters, optimizing communication resources while ensuring microgrid stability.
  • Conducted experiments and validated the control methods using a hardware-in-the-loop testbed (Typhoon HIL and dSPACE).
  • Contributed to a paper published in IEEE Internet of Things Journal, DOI: 10.1109/JIOT.2023.3305279.

Project 2: Performance limitation analysis and data-driven control of DC Microgrids subject to uncertain disruptions (Funded by Office of Naval Research)

  • Analyzed failure conditions of microgrids, designed a resilient power buffer control method to enhance resilience of boost converters in DC microgrids.
  • Formulated a resilient control method to effectively mitigate the effects of cyber-attacks on microgrid systems.
  • Employed statistical methods to create a regression model of a large-scale microgrid system, analyzing system performance under varying parameters.
  • Utilized Orthogonal Fractional Factorial Designs and Taguchi design-of-experiments method to optimize performance and efficiency.
  • Contributed to IEEE Control Systems Letters (DOI: 10.1109/LCSYS.2023.3341727) and IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems I (DOI: 10.1109/TCSI.2024.3415966).

Project 3: Transitioning to Ultra-Resilient Microgrids through AI, Advanced Control and High-Speed Sensors (Funded by Office of Naval Research)

  • Developed a data-driven reinforcement-learning control strategy to enhance system performance under uncertain disturbances.
  • Formulated a game-theoretical methodology integrated with optimal control for microgrid planning and decision-making.
  • Created an uncertainty evaluation strategy for large-scale uncertain systems based on sampling methods.
  • Analyzed NREL data to capture system statistics and developed an online uncertainty evaluation method for microgrid systems.
